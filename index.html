About Me

I'm an AI Engineer and Data Scientist with a passion for turning complex data into compelling stories. With over 4 years of hands-on experience in data analysis, machine learning, and MLOps, I thrive on transforming raw numbers into actionable insights that drive innovation and business growth.

My professional journey began with a Master’s degree in Information and Electronic Engineering, which laid the foundation for my expertise in data analysis, automation, and real-time process monitoring. Over the years, I've contributed to diverse projects—from developing AI-powered chatbots that streamline production processes at Albert Handtmann Maschinenfabrik GmbH & Co. KG, to crafting robust ML pipelines that unlock hidden patterns in large datasets at Keysight Technologies and Magnificent Data GmbH.

When I'm not immersed in coding and model building, I enjoy exploring new topics to learn and stay up to date within the community. I often experiment with personal projects that push the boundaries of what's possible with data. My approach is holistic: I start by gathering data, then apply rigorous analysis and machine learning techniques, and finally deliver insights in a form that’s accessible to everyone—from technical teams to business decision-makers.

I invite you to explore my work in the Portfolio and Resume sections, where you'll find detailed project showcases and a comprehensive overview of my professional achievements. Whether you're here to collaborate, learn, or simply share a passion for data, I believe that the intersection of storytelling and data is where the magic happens. Welcome to my world of data-driven innovation.
